Other Ways to Engage

THE CONNECTOR

Virtually connecting teachers with industry professionals in every classroom. Each session is created by teachers to have volunteers speak about a variety of requested topics. Check out a brief video of the Connector in action or an overview flyer to get more information. Note that the platform will require creating a free account and a free virtual background check to be completed (conducted by a third party with no cost to the volunteer). Please contact Britanny Verrette for questions at bverrette@prepckc.org.

Don't see an in-person event or session on The Connector that works for you? Fill out this form and we will connect with you about future opportunities.

ADDITIONAL OPPORTUNITIES

Interested in becoming more involved? We seek support for Workplace Visits and Client-Connected Projects from local organizations and businesses. Please click here for more information and how to get involved.

VOLUNTEER ORIENTATION

We encourage all volunteers to participate in our Virtual DEI Volunteer Orientation held twice in the fall and spring semesters. This virtual volunteer orientation via Zoom will help new, current, and prospective volunteers learn about our mission, the importance of diversity, equity, and inclusion, and your role as the volunteer. Available Orientation sessions will be listed among the events below.

When you sign up to volunteer, our schools are counting on you to attend. If something comes up, we completely understand but if possible please let us know in advance or help find a substitute.
