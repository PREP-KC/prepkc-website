Frequently Asked Questions

What time commitment is required for volunteering?

Most volunteer opportunities range from 1-3 hours. We offer flexible scheduling to accommodate your availability.

Do I need teaching experience to volunteer?

No teaching experience is required! We provide all necessary training and materials. Your professional experience is what students need to hear about.

Which Kansas City schools does PREP-KC serve?

We partner with 6 school districts and 4 charter schools across the Kansas City metropolitan area, serving over 67,000 students in Missouri and Kansas.

How can my company get involved?

Companies can sponsor events, provide speakers, host field trips, or partner on career pathway programs. Contact us to discuss corporate partnership opportunities.
