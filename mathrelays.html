What's Math Relay

Math Relays is a competition that is held in the fall of each year. This competition is held between multiple different schools to see who's students can come out on top. The competition is held in a variety of different categories including Pre-Algebra, Algebra I, Geometry, Algebra II. The competition is held in a variety of different levels from elementary to high school.



Schools will be awarded a plaque that can be displayed in their school identifying them as a Gold, Silver or Bronze level school based on their score. All student participants will receive a medal that corresponds to the Gold, Silver and Bronze places earned by the school. The district that receives the highest percentage of points based on number of participants will also receive a traveling trophy. The winners are determined by a calculation of points earned in each competition.

High School Relays

Math Relays 2022 at Grandview High School marks its 13th year with exciting updates. The event now features a new testing platform for individual tests, requiring students to bring their devices. Additionally, hands-on team events, such as an escape room, amazing race, or super medley event, make a return from the virtual sessions. Coaches can enroll using the provided form.

What's New
