Data In Action Events

PREP-KC supports career exploration opportunities in a variety of formats for all ages. Each event is listed below for volunteers to sign up as they are available. We appreciate your careful consideration of your availability and thank you for making an impact in our students' lives!

Data Dialogue Events

Come join us as a participant in our Data Dialogue event with 8th grade students. Set up like speed networking, but for careers, you will speak with multiple small groups of students about your career pathway, how you use data in your career and how school is relevant to their future. The goal is to help expose students to a wide range of data influenced careers, to help develop their career aspirations and plans for their future.

Please check back later for upcoming Data Dialogue events.

Project Mentor Sessions

Want to help students who are learning about the data science process? PREP-KC is looking for industry professionals to donate an hour of their time to serve as Project Mentors.

For the final project, our Data In Action! students will be telling stories with data by building Tableau storyboards. We are looking for Project Mentors to provide kind, specific and helpful feedback, helping students move forward with their projects. No specific expertise is required to be a Project Mentor. The PREP-KC team will provide just-in-time training!

Please check back later for upcoming Project Mentor sessions.
