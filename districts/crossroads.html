About the Events

Crossroads School District partners with PREP‑KC to connect professionals with our K‑12 students for hands‑on career exploration. Whether you can spare an hour or a day, your expertise sparks curiosity and opens doors for hundreds of young learners.

We understand life gets busy—should plans change, please let us know as soon as possible and, if you can, suggest a colleague to step in. Our students count on these life‑changing experiences. Thank you!
