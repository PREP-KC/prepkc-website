Project 1: PREP-KC Website Redesign

Website Development

Our students took on the ambitious project of redesigning and deploying the new prepkc.org website. Embracing the latest web technologies, they utilized HTML, CSS, and Bootstrap to create a contemporary and responsive web presence for PREP-KC.

The project was not without its challenges. At the onset of the spring semester, we faced a significant outage that rendered the existing site inoperative. Displaying remarkable initiative and tenacity, our students stepped up to the challenge. They collaborated intensively to write the necessary code, troubleshoot issues, and launch the revamped website in less than a week.

This rapid development and deployment exemplify the real-world skills and problem-solving abilities that PREP-KC instills in its students. It's a testament to their hard work and dedication to learning and applying coding skills in high-pressure situations.

Project 2: Pathful Connect Data Collection and Reporting Tool

Leveraging a robust stack consisting of Flask, SQLite, HTML, and HTMX, our students have developed a CRUD application essential for managing data from virtual sessions conducted with professionals and students across the metro area.

This tool serves a dual purpose by equipping districts with tailored reporting features and providing PREP-KC with internal tools for utilizing the Pathful Connect platform effectively.
