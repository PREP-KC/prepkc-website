Project Overview

Project Objective:

Analyze and provide insights on PREP-KC's recent event feedback data to identify key areas of success and opportunities for improvement.

Pick at least one topic such as participant satisfaction, event impact, or areas needing enhancement.

Data Analysis:

You should complete a thorough exploration of the dataset provided. Identify patterns, trends, outliers, or correlations in the data.

Start by forming hypotheses or questions based on initial data exploration and using the data to support or refute these.

Visualization and Presentation
  • For projects using Tableau or similar tools, expect clear, informative, and aesthetically pleasing visualizations that effectively communicate the data's story.(At least 2 visuals)
  • For PowerPoint/Canva presentations, look for a well-structured narrative that guides the audience through the analysis, supported by data visualizations, statistics, and insights. (At least 5 slides)
  • For written analyses, expect clarity, depth, and coherence in the narrative, with appropriate inclusion of charts or tables to illustrate key points. (At least one page)
Recommendations:

Provide insightful observations based on their data analysis. These should go beyond mere descriptions of the data to include interpretations and implications.

Focus on actionable recommendations or suggestions for PREP-KC based on their findings. These should be practical, data-driven, and clearly linked to the analysis.
