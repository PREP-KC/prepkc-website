About

PREP-KC partners with 6 districts and 4 charters enrolling over 67,000 students. Representing the culturally rich, diverse, talented, communities across the heart of Kansas City.

  • 80% students of color
  • 84% low-income students
  • 3,800 annual high school graduates
  • 21 high schools and 23 middle schools
  • 19 of the 20 zip codes with the lowest median household incomes
