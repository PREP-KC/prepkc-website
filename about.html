About

PREP-KC partners with 6 districts and 4 charters enrolling over 67,000 students across the Kansas City metropolitan area. As a leading Missouri nonprofit, we represent the culturally rich, diverse, talented communities throughout the heart of Kansas City.

  • 80% students of color
  • 84% low-income students
  • 3,800 annual high school graduates
  • 21 high schools and 23 middle schools across Kansas City
  • 19 of the 20 zip codes with the lowest median household incomes in Missouri
